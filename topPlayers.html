Fun Facts

Did you know that Francesco Totti played for Roma for his entire career?

Totti was born in Roma, and he was always a big fan of the team. He always wanted to play for them. He joined the team in 1983, and he retired in 2017, solely playing for AS Roma eventhough teams like Barcelona had tried to offer him a contract.

Did you know that AS Roma shares the Stadium with their worst rival?

Roma's biggest rival is Lazio team which are also located in Rome. Everytime there is a match between them, they give their best to prove to the city which team is better.

Did you know that Mohamed Salah used to play for AS Roma?

Before Salah played for Liverpool, he was playing for Roma. He is an extraordinary player. He joined Roma in 2015, left for Liverpool in 2017.
